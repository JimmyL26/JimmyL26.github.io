These are some great restaurants to check out!

99 Favor Taste

From their website: "99 Favor Taste is a all-you-can-eat hotpot and bbq restaurant in NYC. We offer 6 choices of hot pot broths, which serve as perfect soup bases for 31 different kinds of fresh vegetable and meat. 99 Favor Taste is a place that can bring you great fulfillment and endless enjoy."

Four Main Locations:

  • Brooklyn: 732 61st Street, Brooklyn, NY 11220
  • Manhattan: 285 Grand Street, New York, NY 10002
  • Queens: 135-41 37th Avenue, Flushing, NY 11354
  • Staten Island: 201 New Dorp Ln, Staten Island, NY 10306
99 Favors Taste Offical Website

Ramen Danbo

Ramen Danbo offers customers the taste of authentic Japenense Ramen. They have a variety of Tonkotsu ramen such as Shio Ramen and Miso Ramen. Different toppings and gyoza could also be added on to make the meal even better.

Two Main Locations:

  • 52 7th Ave., Brooklyn, NY USA 11217
  • 48 Carmine St., New York, NY USA 10014
Ramen Danbo Taste Offical Website

Prince Street Pizza

Pizza is the most iconic food in New York as it is quick to eat and relatively cheap. Prince Street Pizza is well known for their square pizzas, especially pepperoni.

One Main Location:

  • Manhattan: 27 Prince St New York, NY 10012
Prince Street Pizza Official Website

Burger By Day

Burgur by Day is a burger joint that is popular for its Smash burger. The environment is very friendly, making it is the perfect place to eat and hangout with friends.

One Main Location:

  • Manhattan: 242 Grand St, New York, NY 10002
