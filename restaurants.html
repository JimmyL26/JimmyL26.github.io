These are some great restaurants to check out!

99 Favor Taste

99 Favor Taste is an all-you-can-eat hotpot and bbq restaurant in NYC. They offer 6 choices of hot pot broths, which serve as perfect soup bases for 31 different kinds of fresh vegetable and meat. When having bbq, the customer can order an endless amount of meat of their choosing and even cook it to their liking using the grill at the table.

Four Main Locations:

  • Brooklyn: 732 61st Street, Brooklyn, NY 11220
  • Manhattan: 285 Grand Street, New York, NY 10002
  • Queens: 135-41 37th Avenue, Flushing, NY 11354
  • Staten Island: 201 New Dorp Ln, Staten Island, NY 10306

Ramen Danbo

Ramen Danbo offers customers the taste of authentic Japenense Ramen. They have a variety of Tonkotsu ramen such as Shio Ramen and Miso Ramen where different toppings can be added based on the customers preferences. Gyoza could also be added on as a side dish to make the meal even more filling.

Two Main Locations:

  • 52 7th Ave., Brooklyn, NY USA 11217
  • 48 Carmine St., New York, NY USA 10014
Ramen Danbo Taste Offical Website

Prince Street Pizza

Pizza is the most iconic food in New York as it is quick to eat and relatively cheap. Prince Street Pizza is a great example of a restaurant having delicious pizza. They are well known for their square pizzas, especially their pepperoni filled ones. They also have normal slices to meet everyone's tastes.

One Main Location:

  • Manhattan: 27 Prince St New York, NY 10012
Prince Street Pizza Official Website

Burger By Day

Burgur by Day is a burger joint that is popular for its Smash burger. They are heavy on fast food items such as burgers and fries but they are all made better in terms of appearance and taste. The environment is very friendly, making it is the perfect place to eat and hangout with friends.

One Main Location:

  • Manhattan: 242 Grand St, New York, NY 10002
